Throughout my experience at the Apple Developer Academy, I had the privilege of creating "RunOut," a game inspired by Northeastern Brazilian culture and the art of Cordel woodcut. With simple and charming illustrations, "RunOut" invites players to accompany Tropi on his journey home after a long day of work, overcoming obstacles along the way. As they dive into this adventure, players not only have fun but also learn about the culture and traditions of Northeastern Brazil while reflecting on the importance of balancing work and personal life.
